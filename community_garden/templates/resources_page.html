Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my local community garden?

You can find your local community garden through the map locator on the Home page, or select it from the list of community gardens.

How can I volunteer for my local community garden?

Sign up through our login system and volunteer by first selecting your desired garden and then pressing the 'Volunteer' button. Please note that you must have an account created with us.

How can I donate to my local community garden?

Locate your desired garden and then press the 'Donate' button, where you will be able to make a monetary or wishlist donation.

How can I register my local community garden?

You can register your own garden by first creating a profile by clicking on "Sign Up" tab. After creating your profile, you will be able to locate a tab called "Register a Garden"
